### 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites function properly and remember your preferences.

| Google (Analytics) | Website analytics (disabled) | [Google's Cookie Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies) |

---

### 6. How to Manage Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

| Browser | Instructions |
|---------|---------------|
| Chrome | Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies |
| Safari | Preferences → Privacy → Manage website data |
| Firefox | Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and site data |
| Edge | Settings → Cookies and site permissions |

**Note:** Blocking essential cookies may break the map functionality.

---

### 7. Your Cookie Choices

When you visit our site, you can:
- **Accept all cookies** - Allows essential and non-essential cookies
- **Reject non-essential** - Only essential cookies (currently: just Google Maps)
