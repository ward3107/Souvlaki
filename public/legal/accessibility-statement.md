## English

**Last Updated: January 17, 2026**

Greek Souvlaki Kfar Yasif is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities.

### Accessibility Features
Our website includes the following accessibility features:

1. **Multi-Language Support**
   - Available in Hebrew, Arabic, Russian, Greek, and English
   - RTL (Right-to-Left) support for Hebrew and Arabic

2. **Text Size Adjustment**
   - Increase/decrease text size (90% - 150%)
   - Readable font option for better readability

3. **Color & Contrast**
   - High contrast mode for better visibility
   - Grayscale mode for color blindness
   - Invert colors option

4. **Navigation Assistance**
   - Reading guide (horizontal line following cursor)
   - Big cursor option
   - Highlight links option

5. **Content Adjustments**
   - Stop animations option
   - Keyboard navigation support

### How to Use
1. Click the accessibility icon (bottom left/right corner)
2. Adjust settings to your needs
3. Settings are saved for future visits
